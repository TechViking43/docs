## How Onboarding Works

Getting from sign-up to a live, automated workflow takes four straightforward steps. Follow them in order to have your first automation running as quickly as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Head to [app.quantumflow.ai](https://app.quantumflow.ai) and create your free account. Verify your email address to unlock full platform access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Tools">
    Link the apps your team already uses — Slack, Gmail, HubSpot, Salesforce, and dozens more — through the Integrations panel in your dashboard. QuantumFlow AI uses OAuth and secure API key storage so your credentials stay safe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Your First Workflow">
    Open the Workflow Builder and drag-and-drop your triggers, AI agent steps, and actions into a sequence. Use one of the pre-built templates to get a working automation in minutes, or start from a blank canvas for full control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Live">
    Activate your workflow with a single toggle. Monitor real-time task execution, review logs, and iterate — all from one unified dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
